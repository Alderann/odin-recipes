The best lasagna recipe

Description

This is a simple lasagna recipe. An easy dinner that everyone will enjoy.

Please remember that everyone will ask for another plate, so prepare enough!

Ingredients
  • 12 wholes lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounces) of packaged cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup of shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 1/2 cups of tomato-basil pasta sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagne noodles and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente. Drain.
  3. Meanwhile, place ground beef, garlic, oregano, garlic powder, salt and black pepper in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir until beef is crumbly and evenly browned (about 10 minutes).
  4. Mix cottage cheese, parmesan cheese and eggs together in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.
  5. Lay 4 noodles side by side on the bottom of a 9 x 13 inches baking pan. Top with a layer of preparer tomato-basil sauce, a layer of ground beef mixture and a layer of the cheese mixture. Repeat layers twice more, ending with a layer of sauce. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 30 minutes or until the lasagna is bubbling and the cheese has melted. Remove foil and bake until cheese has begun to brown. Allow to stand at least 10 minutes before serving.
